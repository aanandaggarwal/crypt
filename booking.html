Make Your Event Legendary

Interested in bringing your sound to The Crypt? We're excited to host a variety of events from local rock and indie concerts to drag shows.

Please contact us at least two weeks in advance to schedule your performance. Here's what you need to know:

  • Capacity: 150 people max
  • Stage Specs: 20 feet x 15 feet
  • Sound: Basic PA system provided, bands must bring their own amp/special equipment.
  • Lighting: Standard stage lighting with basic effects
