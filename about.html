About The Crypt

Discover the origins and journey of The Crypt, your local jam spot born from a collective passion for music and community.

Notable Events

  • 2019 - The Beginning

    The Crypt started as a living space for a group of musicians. They would jam at all hours of day and night, frequently inviting friends and local artists to join them during sessions.

  • 2020 - Community Expansion

    As word spread, The Crypt became a community hub, hosting weekly music events, and attracting talent from all over the region. Our operations evolved, opening up the backyard, living room, and downstairs bar.

  • 2021 - Official Recognition

    The Crypt became locally recognized as an indie hotspot, growing in popularity thanks to word-of-mouth and local show group chats. We began our entry stamp system, aiming to better regulate max capacity.

  • 2022 - Beyond Music

    Broadening its scope, The Crypt began to include other forms of art, such as visual exhibitions, spoken word, and drag shows. We hosted our first ever drag performance, and it was a resounding success. The support and warmth of the community has helped make drag a staple of the Crypt experience.

  • 2023 - Today

    Continuing to thrive as a cornerstone of music culture, The Crypt remains dedicated to fostering creativity and building a supportive artist community. Our venue welcomes all, inviting you to leave your expectations at the door and share amazing moments.
